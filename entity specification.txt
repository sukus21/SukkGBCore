Mandatory entity information:
10 bytes for vars
1 byte for collision mask
3 bytes for extended data pointer
2 bytes for step function

Entity sizes:
half-size: 16-bytes
full-size: 32-bytes
double-size: 64 bytes

Per-entity fields:
* Damage
* Damage 2
* Also damage
* Colour (aka Damage)
* Ouchiness
* Agony
* Damage
* Pain-scale
